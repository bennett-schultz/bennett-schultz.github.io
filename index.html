About

From Plainfield, Illinois. Built a genuine interest in finance early and started a personal portfolio as soon as possible. Markets are followed each morning and free time is spent building investment research for personal positions.

Interests include dirt biking, baseball, and cars, especially Ferrari and Aston Martin.

Investment Philosophy

Equities | Growth | Turnarounds | Catalysts

Equities are treated as the strongest long term compounding vehicle and the portfolio is intentionally weighted toward them at this stage. Research centers on high quality growth names and turnaround situations where catalysts are clear, understandable, and communicable. Focus is placed on setups where pessimism appears overextended and sentiment can shift meaningfully due to contracts, policy, or macro positioning, particularly across aerospace and space adjacent names.
