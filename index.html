About

From Plainfield, Illinois. Developed an early interest in finance and started a personal investment portfolio as soon as it was possible. Follows markets each morning and builds research in free time to support personal positions, focusing on clear catalysts and explainable theses.

Interests include dirt biking, baseball, and cars, especially Ferrari and Aston Martin.

Investment Philosophy

Equities | Growth | Turnarounds | Catalysts

Equities are treated as the strongest long term compounding vehicle, and the portfolio is intentionally weighted toward them at this stage. Research centers on two categories: high quality growth names and turnaround situations where catalysts are clear, understandable, and communicable. A repeated focus is on businesses where near term pessimism appears overextended and price action reflects more fear than fundamental impairment. Special attention is paid to sentiment inflection setups tied to policy, contracts, and macro positioning, particularly across aerospace and space adjacent names.
